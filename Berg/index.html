Hello, My Name is Ben Zelinski!

I'm a Software Engineer living in Cleveland, OH. I'm currently seeking remote employment.

My ability to quickly adapt and excel has given me the opportunity to explore and thrive in a wide variety of skills and industries. I've run a million-dollar Napa Valley wine program, co-founded a bakery in the heart of NYC, made wine, and been an ASE-certified mechanic.

Throughout these ventures, I've repeatedly been tasked with training and leading teams, as well as developing or revamping internal systems to improve efficiency and productivity. My 'casually professional' approach to leadership allows me to engage with, protect, and motivate those around me.

My software development philosophy is similarly people-centric. I pride myself on being a team player, which means writing code that is resilient, easy to parse and maintain, and most importantly, written with an eye towards the overall team goals.
